Different assets will have varying spreads based on two factors:

Liquidity enables you to quickly buy or sell an asset without causing major changes to the price. The higher the liquidity of an asset, the lower the spread and risk of price swings.

Volatility measures how rapidly the price changes. For example crypto assets are much more volatile than forex pairs therefore they tend to have wider spreads. Bitcoin can easily jump 10% in a day while EUR/USD usually needs weeks for such gains.
