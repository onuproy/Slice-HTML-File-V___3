Secret to Price Movements

4 min read

In this chapter, we’ll dig deep and uncover the fundamental principle that makes prices move and thus creates opportunities for traders.

This will help you understand what drives trends and huge profitable trades beneath the surface.

Traders who learn this can build enormous wealth while others stay two steps behind, wondering why the chart always moves against them.

Every move on the chart is based in the economic principle of supply and demand❗

Buyers of assets create demand (buying pressure) and sellers of assets create supply(selling pressure).

When supply matches demand, it is known as consolidation or indecision between buyers and sellers. This is not an ideal scenario for traders who aim to profit from price swings.

The most profitable moves happen when buyers and sellers are out of balance 👇

The psychology behind trends 💡

Downtrends happen when more people are selling but not many are buying, creating excess "supply". A lack of buyers means the price has to come down until buyers gain interest again.

Uptrends happen when everyone wants to buy, but not many people are selling. This means that sellers can ask buyers to pay more, thus driving the price up.

All that trading is, is a constant battle between supply and demand, sellers and buyers.
